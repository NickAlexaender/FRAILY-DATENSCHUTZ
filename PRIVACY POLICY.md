PRIVACY POLICY – FRAILY

Protecting your personal data is important to us. With this Privacy Policy, we inform you about which data is processed when using the Fraily app, for what purpose this is done, and which rights you have under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Controller

The controller responsible for data processing within the meaning of the GDPR is:

Fraily
(Nick Lehmacher, Gerhart-Hauptmann Str. 45a, 90513 Zirndorf, Germany)

2. Principles of Data Processing

Fraily processes personal data exclusively in accordance with legal requirements, in particular the GDPR. Personal data is only collected if this is necessary for providing, using, and further developing the App.

Personal data is not shared or sold for advertising purposes.

3. Categories of Data Processed

When using Fraily, the following types of data may be processed in particular:

User Data

User ID

Profile information (e.g. name or user-defined labels)

App-related settings

Content Data

Texts, notes, messages, and other content created by the user

Text suggestions or content generated by the App

Technical Data

Device information (e.g. operating system, app version)

Times of app access

Firebase-specific identifiers

Push Notification Data

Device and/or push token for sending notifications

4. Purpose of Data Processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

Provision and operation of the App

Storage and management of user-generated content

Supporting users in maintaining contacts and relationships

Generating texts and messages using AI-supported features

Sending push notifications (e.g. reminders or notices)

Ensuring the technical stability and security of the App

Further development and improvement of App features

5. Use of Firebase (Google)

Fraily uses services provided by Google Firebase, a backend service of Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland.

Firebase is used in particular for:

Data storage (e.g. Firestore or Realtime Database)

User authentication

Cloud Functions for app logic

Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications

All user-created content and user-related data is stored and processed in Firebase.

Google may also process data in third countries (in particular the USA). Data transfers are carried out on the basis of the Standard Contractual Clauses provided by Google in accordance with Art. 46 GDPR.

Further information on data protection at Google Firebase can be found in Google’s privacy policy.

6. Use of ChatGPT (OpenAI)

Fraily uses ChatGPT, an AI service provided by OpenAI, to generate or support texts, messages, and content suggestions.

For this purpose, user-provided content (e.g. text inputs) is transmitted to OpenAI insofar as this is necessary for the respective function. Processing is carried out solely for the purpose of providing the requested AI function.

Fraily does not use the transmitted data for advertising purposes. Processing by OpenAI is subject to OpenAI’s own privacy policies.

7. Push Notifications

Fraily uses push notifications to inform users about relevant events or reminders. Notifications are sent via Firebase Cloud Messaging.

For this purpose, a device-related push token is processed. Push notifications can be disabled at any time via the device settings.

8. Legal Bases for Processing

Personal data is processed on the basis of the following legal grounds:

Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR – processing for performance of a contract / use of the App

Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR – legitimate interest in secure, functional, and user-friendly app operation

Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR – consent, insofar as this is required (e.g. push notifications)

9. Storage Period

Personal data is stored only as long as necessary for the purposes stated above or as required by statutory retention obligations.

Users may request deletion of their account and the associated data at any time.

10. Rights of Data Subjects

Users have the right at any time to:

Request information about their stored data (Art. 15 GDPR)

Request correction of inaccurate data (Art. 16 GDPR)

Request deletion of their data (Art. 17 GDPR)

Request restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)

Object to processing (Art. 21 GDPR)

Request data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

Requests can be addressed to the controller.

11. Data Security

Fraily implements technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against loss, manipulation, or unauthorized access. Data transmission is encrypted.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the App evolves or due to legal changes. The current version shall apply in each case.
